FEDERONI

Federated only, we're Family...

Ma che cosa é?

Federoni is a federated-only social surname. The Federoni family is the web's first social network that looks like it exists from the outside, but it doesn't.

We interact with other federated social networks like identi.ca thanks to the OStatus protocol, but there is no web 2.0 application here. There's nothing behind this page. Not even for members.

Our Federoni identities live here, but we only operate them using unhosted web apps. Most members of the Federoni family actively contribute to the Unhosted project.
